## Key Syntheses (User's Original Insights)

1. Nietzsche is a **constructive metaphysician** (§36), mischaracterised as anti-metaphysics
2. Part One = clearing reader's prejudices, *preparing* for constructive philosophy (not mere demolition)
3. §§2–4 (falsity/envy/justice) = foreshadowing of the entire revaluation project
4. Nature-critique §9 → psychology-critique §23 via genealogical method
5. §1 will-to-truth → §11 Kant-critique = architectural arc across Part One
6. §36: material world = "more primitive form of world of emotions"
