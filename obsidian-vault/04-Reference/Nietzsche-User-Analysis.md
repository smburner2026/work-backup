## Key Findings

A systematic analysis of how the user reads Nietzsche. Extracts intellectual style, curiosity patterns, and how they connect ideas.

### Intellectual Style
- Systematic and methodical — reads section-by-section, returns to connect threads
- Evidence-hungry — demands textual proof, specific passages
- Connection-seeker — traces thematic links, never isolated points
- Intellectually humble but assertive — corrects confidently when wrong
- Metacognitive — reflects on their own understanding process

### Reading Approach
- Probes for hidden architecture, not surface content
- Connects BGAE across Nietzsche's corpus (GS, Z, TI, Nachlass)
- Takes productive diversions (e.g., Alamariu's esoteric-Plato thesis)
- Builds cumulative frameworks, sees dialectical relationships
- Brings historical context (Kant, German Idealism, Stendhal, French Revolution)

### What They Want From a Text
- Not paraphrase but *architecture* — how the pieces fit together
- Not mere understanding but *orientation* — where the author is going
- Not passive reception but *conversation* — push back, test, connect
